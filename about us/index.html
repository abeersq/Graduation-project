OUR STORY

Our story depends on students in the department of computer science who have problems borrowing from graduation projects. The most important point is the repetition of graduation project topics due to a lack of full knowledge, and because there are a large number of graduation projects in the department of computer science, the department needs an archiving system that combines all graduation projects, with the ability to add or modify projects and communicate with the supervisor. The system also provides the progress status of the graduation project for each group.

Service We Provide

Among the services provided by the website to students and faculty members.

Graduation projects management

Our department keeps all GP file records as a soft copy and hard copy. Students can now see all kinds of GP files Whether software or research at any time. to allow student and faculty to benefit from previous projects.

Connecting students with supervisors

The website can be connecting to your supervisor easy to continue the graduation project course and if you have any problem you need to help with the supervisor .

Easy and quick access to GP file

An important feature on this website is quick access to the GP file can Students review to help you continue the GP1/GP2 course, and see previously developed projects to get an idea of ​​the types of projects in their area of ​​interest.
